Customer may use the Platform solely for Customer’s business purposes. Customer is solely responsible for (a)

through the Service; (d) providing for and maintaining any systems, software, hardware, web browser and
Internet service necessary to access and use the Platform, including current versions of web browser programs
supported by EC; and (e) establishing, managing, maintaining, and supporting access and report routing rules,

communications and any other distribution rules regarding the Platform or information contained therein.

Customer and its employees or representatives shall not (a) modify, make derivative works of, reverse engineer,

symbols, or labels in the Platform.

EC may from time to time at its discretion make available to its Customers one or more optional products,
